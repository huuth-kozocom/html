PICKUP NFTs LINEUP

This sale offers three types of collaboration limited edition NFTs: two GENESIS collaboration NFTs and one XENO collaboration NFT. The collaboration limited edition NFT will not be reproduced or duplicated on the PROJECT XENO network in the future.

  • The two GENESIS collaboration NFTs (GENESIS Ver. I and GENESIS Ver. II) are the same as GENESIS<FIRST> and will have the effect that increases the number of tokens earned the most.
  • The XENO collaboration NFTs will have the same performance as the regular XENO NFTs but will be designed with a limited issuance quantity.

In addition to the normal XENO ability, this XENO comes standard with a GENESIS-specific passive skill that enhances the "ability to EARN," allowing the user to earn more tokens in battle. There are five levels from FIRST to FIFTH, with FIRST being the design with the lowest issuance volume and the largest number of tokens. It features a masked design and is an extremely rare NFT with a maximum number of tokens issued per class.
This drawing will feature all classes from FIRST to FIFTH, a total of 30 types.

  • When a new class is added, a new class GENESIS will be issued.

XENO is an NFT equipped with the "ability to earn" as standard, and winning battles earns tokens.
XENO is generated from tens of thousands of different designs and six classes, BREAKER /SAMURAI /GUARDIAN /PSYCHIC /NINJA /GRAPPLER, with two passive skills and one special move randomly assigned and generated.

Charms are NFTs that can be equipped on XENO to change its appearance and provide additional passive skills.
It has three parts: head, body, and tail.

A total of nine types will be available for each part in this drawing, one for each rarity of EPIC, RARE, and COMMON.

Weapons are NFTs that can be equipped on XENO to change its appearance and enhance the effects of its skill cards.
Each class of XENO will have its own Weapon.

A total of eighteen types will be available for each part in this drawing, one for each rarity of EPIC, RARE, and COMMON.

FAQ

  • What is an NFT?

    It refers to digital data with ownership information.
    For example, art images or game character images are set to who they belong to and can be bought and sold.

  • What is a Wallet?

    A wallet for crypto assets (virtual currency) is "a wallet dedicated to crypto assets (virtual currency)" and refers to a place where crypto assets (virtual currency) are stored.

  • How can I receive NFT after purchase?

    You can check your earned NFTs on MY PAGE (https://mypage.project-xeno.com/) on the PROJECT XENO official website.
    Your NFT acquisition information is maintained at the address of the wallet you connected to at the time of purchase.
    Therefore, it is required to connect with the same wallet you used at the time of purchase.

    • Confirmation of your purchased NFT design is scheduled for around January 2023.
    • Please wait a certain amount of time after purchase to receive your NFT.
    • The actual NFTs will be available between the marketplace launch and the game launch.

  • Can I buy or sell the NFTs I won?

    It is possible to buy and sell NFTs issued by PROJECT XENO all over the world, including NFTs acquired in this sale, on PROJECT XENO's original marketplace.

    However, the following NFTs will not be available for trading:

    • Common rarity weapons and charms
    • The actual NFTs will be available between the marketplace launch and the game launch.
    • Please note that the specs may change depending on the development status.

  • What wallet should I use?

    We recommend MetaMask (https://metamask.io/) as the most well-known and secure, but other wallets are also acceptable as long as they are compatible with WalletConnect.
